MOTIVATION

CIPHS is an experimental Cryptocurrency base on ERC20/223 Ethereum Token. By taking advantage of Ethereum Blockchain, CIPHS were invented to acted as Cryptocurrency with underlying unique core function for integration with other valuable dApps project such as Gaming, Banking, Website, Shopping and even Asset Issuing. Regardless of simple or even complex implemention, CIPHS is in value of dAPPS rapid development within worldwideweb and fintech. A Cryptocurrency backed by Community, built for Everyone. CIPHS creation as on 1st proposal with guideline to ensure safest decentralize cryptocurrency payment system.
