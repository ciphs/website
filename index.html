Getting CIPHS is Simple

Directly send Ether from your favorite ERC20 wallets, CIPHS will be auto convert based on 1:1 ratio to ETH, flexibility for investment regardless of current market price. As in return, the Investor's wallet are eligible to receive new minted CIPHS upon succesful proposal of additional total supply as a method of distribution into circulation (do not send Ether from exchange wallet, you have been warned).. Or you could simply buy CIPHS over the major exchange market (at market price) for daily usage.
