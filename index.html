## MOTIVATION CIPHS is an experimental Autonomous Decentralize Cryptocurrency with unique function to be use for integration with other valuable projects regardless of complex or simple implemention. CIPHS were invented to acted as cryptocurrency with underlying function such as tracking, voting, investing, burning and banning. CIPHS is a cash currency, but in digital hybridization way.
